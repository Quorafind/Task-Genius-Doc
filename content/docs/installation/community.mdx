---
title: Community Installation
---

1.  Open `Settings` -> `Community plugins` in Obsidian.
2.  Make sure "Restricted mode" is **off**.
3.  Click `Browse` community plugins.
4.  Search for "Task Genius".
5.  Click `Install`.
6.  Once installed, find "Task Genius" in your installed plugins list and toggle it on to `Enable` the plugin.

## Notice

You might need to run the command `Task Genius: Force reindex all tasks` to reindex your tasks after installing the plugin from the community plugins list.
